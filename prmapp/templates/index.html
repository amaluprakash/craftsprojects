PRM Metal Crafts Promoted by PRM Lakshmana Iyer Associates having 123 years of Excellence is completing its Fifteenth year at Ernakulam. They are the largest and oldest Manufacturer of Bronze craft in Kerala based at Mannar, Alapuzha District whose products are well known for its tradition and quality.

The revolutionary Product of PRM Metal crafts is the Vasthuvillaku launched in 2009 which is designed according to the traditional vastu sastra by visualizing all the five elements determining vastu called Aadhara Sakthi, Moola prakriti, Aadhi Kurmam, Ananthan and prathivi(Earth). The belief is that Ananthan (Sarpam)is carrying the Earth since snake forms cannot be included in a lamp the same is represented by "Pavithra Kettu"in front of the lamp. The lamp is made in Panchaloha kootu which when lighted before sunrise believed to eradicate vastu dosha and other shortcomings happened during construction. The Lamp comes in four sizes based in "kol"measurement used for measuring Earth. One Kol is equivalent to 72 cms. The base model 24-cms costs Rs.3500/-, 36-cms Rs.7000/-, 54 cms Rs.17500/- and 72- cms Rs.35000/- including GST. Even though PRM is the only manufacturer for this design registered VASTHU VILAKKU so many duplicate forms have entered the market which shows the acceptance of this lamp said K.Lakshminarayanan,owner of the shop.

PRM Metal crafts also deals with a wide range of products such as the original Aranmulla mirror, Shanku(both Edampiri and Valampiri), good collection of Statues in gold and Antique, Hand made Puja set items, Lakshmi lamp, para sets, Pancha patra and Udharani, Thooku villaku, Thazhika kudam, Kavara and pathi lamps, Nadan kindi, Kalasa kudom in copper and brass, Deepasthamba lamps, varieties of items used in temples, polishing and exchange of old brass items etc. Mannar church bells are also famous for clarity and sweetness of sound. Hand made Urulis and big varpu used for cooking in marriage functions are also in great demand. We are also dealers of pithambari shining powder and their other products.
